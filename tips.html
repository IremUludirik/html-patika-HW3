= Profiterole Recipe =

How Many Persons:

8-10 Persons

           

Preparation Time:

20 min

           

Cooking Time:

45 min

           

1 ball of profiteroles:

107 calories


      Products Making Tips                

  • The most important point when making profiteroles is that the eggs are fed in a good way.
    It is the eggs that make the dough rise in profiteroles.
    If your eggs are few, your dough will not rise as much.
    Therefore, you should also be careful that the eggs you use are not small.

  • While feeding the eggs, you should make sure that the dough has cooled down
    and that after breaking the eggs one by one, each one is fed well.

  • You should also consider the possibility of swelling when placing the dough on the tray.

  • When filling the cream, you should be careful that it is warm and not too hot.
    You can use any cream you like. However, it should not be too runny.

  • Here's how you melt chocolate in a bain-marie.
    You put the chocolate in a heatproof bowl and place your bowl in the boiling water.
    So that the chocolate does not come into contact with water.
    The chocolate will melt with the heat it receives from the hot water.
