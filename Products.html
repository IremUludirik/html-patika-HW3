= Profiterole Recipe =

How Many Persons:

8-10 Persons

           

Preparation Time:

20 min

           

Cooking Time:

45 min

           

1 ball of profiteroles:

107 calories


      Products Making Tips                

For Dough

  • 1 glass of water
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 125 g butter or margarine
  • 3 eggs (4 eggs if eggs are small)

For The Cream

  • 2.5 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 1.5 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 packet of vanilla

For The Sauce

  • 1 pack of instant chocolate sauce or 80 g dark chocolate melted in a bain-marie.


































































Bon Appetit!
